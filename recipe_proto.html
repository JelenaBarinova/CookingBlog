Chocolate Homemade Truffles

Posted on:
Dec 30, 2015
Posted by:
Jelena Barinova

Slightly updated and enriched recipe of sweets (Adult version). Easy to make, no baking, you literally just mix everything and that’s all.

Slightly updated and enriched recipe of sweets (Adult version). Easy to make, no baking, you literally just mix everything and that’s all. Definately need to add some more text in here.

  • 35 min

    cooking time

  • 6 Person

    servings

Ingredients

1 can of condensed milk sweetened
360 g of shortbread cookies (or just any cookies you like)
1 handful of nuts (I take pecan)
120 g of dark chocolate
50 g of cacao powder unsweetened
100 g of butter
20 ml of dark rum
of nuts, crached cookies, cacao powder for decoration

Instructions

1: Melt butter and chocolate.
2: Mix crushed cookies and nuts.
3: Add cacao powder to the cookies and nuts, stir well. Start adding condensed milk keep stirring.
4: And finally add melted butter and chocolate.
5: You can add some rum to the mixture at this stage if you like.
6: Refrigerate the mixture for 15 to 30 minutes (the mixture will become thicker and it’s a lot easier to work with it when its cold).
7: Make small balls. Coat them in crushed cookies or cacao powder or crushed nuts.
8: Keep in the fridge before serving.
